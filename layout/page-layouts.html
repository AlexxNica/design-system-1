Top Nav Framework

The utility navigation consists of links and tools that help the user, such as account details, log out, or help information, but are not the core sections of the application.

The primary navigation is used for the main sections of the application or for dropdown categories that organize sections into higher level groups.

The content area is used for your application's main data and interactions.

The footer is used for legal information, application news, and feedback links.
